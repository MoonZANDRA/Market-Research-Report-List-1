<p><p>Targeted Protein Degradation (TPD) is an innovative therapeutic strategy that aims to selectively eliminate malfunctioning or unwanted proteins within cells. By harnessing the body's ubiquitin-proteasome system, TPD leverages small molecules, such as degron-like compounds and PROTACs (proteolysis-targeting chimeras), to bind target proteins and promote their degradation. This approach offers significant advantages over traditional small-molecule inhibitors, as it can facilitate the breakdown of proteins that were previously considered "undruggable."</p><p>The Targeted Protein Degradation Market is currently witnessing robust expansion, driven by advancements in drug discovery technologies and an increasing focus on precision medicine. This convergence of innovative research and increasing investment is poised to fuel significant growth in the TPD landscape, establishing it as a cornerstone of future therapeutic strategies.</p></p>
