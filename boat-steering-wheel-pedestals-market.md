<p><p>The boat steering wheel pedestals market features several types, including pinion, chain, rack-and-pinion, and others. Pinion systems utilize a gear mechanism to convert rotational movement into steering control. Chain systems employ a chain drive for smoother and more powerful steering responses. Rack-and-pinion setups use a toothed rack and gear for precise maneuverability. Other systems may include hydraulic or electronic options, offering varying levels of control and convenience for boat operators, enhancing overall steering performance and user experience.</p></p>
