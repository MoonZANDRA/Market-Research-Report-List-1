<p><ul><li>Air Rotary Coolers</li><li>Water Rotary Coolers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rotary cooler market comprises two primary types: air rotary coolers and water rotary coolers. Air rotary coolers utilize ambient air to dissipate heat from materials, making them suitable for environments with sufficient airflow. They are energy-efficient and often used in industries like mining and metallurgy. Water rotary coolers, on the other hand, employ water for cooling, providing enhanced heat transfer efficiency. These are commonly used in applications requiring precise temperature control, such as chemical processing and power generation.</p></p>
