<p><strong>The Oligonucleotide Synthesis Services Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Primers</li><li>Probes</li><li>Intermediate-scale Synthesis Oligos</li><li>Large-scale Synthesis Oligos</li><li>Linkers and Adaptors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Oligonucleotide synthesis services encompass various categories tailored for research and industrial applications. Primers are short DNA sequences used in PCR for gene amplification. Probes are labeled oligonucleotides necessary for detecting specific nucleic acid sequences. Intermediate-scale synthesis oligos are produced for moderate quantities required in experiments, while large-scale synthesis oligos are generated for widespread applications like therapeutic production. Linkers and adaptors are specialized oligos that facilitate the connection of different biomolecules in various molecular cloning and sequencing processes.</p></p>
