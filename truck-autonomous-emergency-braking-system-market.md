<p><p>The Truck Autonomous Emergency Braking System market comprises three main types: Forward Emergency Braking, Reverse Emergency Braking, and Multi-Directional Emergency Braking. Forward Emergency Braking activates when the vehicle detects an imminent collision ahead, applying brakes to prevent or mitigate impact. Reverse Emergency Braking functions similarly but is designed to prevent accidents while the truck is in reverse, detecting obstacles behind. Multi-Directional Emergency Braking enhances safety by addressing potential threats from various angles, ensuring comprehensive protection in dynamic driving environments.</p></p>
