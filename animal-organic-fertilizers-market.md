<p><p>The animal organic fertilizers market is segmented into dry and liquid types. Dry fertilizers are typically granulated or powdered, making them easy to transport, store, and apply. They release nutrients slowly, enhancing soil health over time. Liquid fertilizers, on the other hand, are easier to absorb by plants and provide immediate nutrient availability. They can be applied through irrigation systems or sprayed directly, allowing for precise nutrient delivery. Both types cater to diverse agricultural needs, promoting sustainable farming practices.</p></p>
