<p><p>The cannabis extract machine market encompasses various extraction methods. Sub/Supercritical CO2 extraction machines utilize CO2 under high pressure to yield pure extracts, favored for their efficiency and safety. Ethanol extraction machines employ ethanol as a solvent, offering effective cannabinoid extraction but requiring careful solvent removal. Hydrocarbon extraction machines use hydrocarbons like butane to produce potent concentrates, but pose safety concerns. Solvent-less extraction machines, such as rosin presses, rely on heat and pressure without solvents, appealing for those seeking pure products. Other methods include various innovative technologies for cannabinoid extraction.</p></p>
