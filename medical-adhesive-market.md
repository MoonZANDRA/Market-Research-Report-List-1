<p><p>The medical adhesive market is categorized into four main types: water-based, solvent-based, solids, and hot melt adhesives. Water-based adhesives utilize water as a solvent, making them eco-friendly and easy to clean. Solvent-based adhesives offer strong bonding through volatile organic compounds but may pose health risks. Solids are typically in a dry form, requiring activation for use, while hot melt adhesives provide rapid bonding upon cooling, ideal for applications requiring quick assembly. Each type serves distinct medical applications, balancing performance with safety.</p></p>
