<p><p>The flood lighting market primarily consists of two types: High Intensity Discharge (HID) and LED flood lighting. HID flood lights, utilizing gas-filled bulbs, provide high brightness and are commonly used in outdoor applications such as sports arenas and street lighting. However, they consume more energy and have shorter lifespans. In contrast, LED flood lighting is energy-efficient, offers longer operational life, and produces minimal heat, making it increasingly popular for both commercial and residential settings.</p></p>
