<p><p>The phenolic wood bonding adhesives market is categorized primarily into granular resin and flaky resin types. Granular resin features a powder-like consistency, allowing for easy mixing and application, resulting in strong adhesion and moisture resistance. Flaky resin, on the other hand, consists of thin, flat particles that provide enhanced surface coverage and bonding strength, particularly in high-performance applications. Both types cater to diverse woodworking needs, offering durability and resistance to environmental factors, making them suitable for industrial and consumer products.</p></p>
