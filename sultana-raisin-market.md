<p><p>Sultana raisins are categorized into two main market types: Natural Dry and Artificial Dry. Natural Dry sultanas are produced by sun-drying grapes, promoting organic processes that preserve flavor and nutrients. In contrast, Artificial Dry sultanas are dehydrated using controlled heat methods, which can accelerate the drying process but may alter taste and texture. Both types cater to diverse consumer preferences, with natural options appealing to those seeking healthier, minimally processed snacks, while artificial variants target convenience and consistency.</p></p>
