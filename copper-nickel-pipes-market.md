<p><p>The Copper-Nickel Pipes market is primarily divided into two types: welded and seamless pipes. Welded pipes are created by joining flat copper-nickel sheets through welding processes, providing durability and flexibility for various applications. Seamless pipes, on the other hand, are manufactured without any joints, offering superior strength and corrosion resistance, making them ideal for high-pressure environments. Both types cater to industries such as marine, oil and gas, and HVAC, emphasizing reliability and performance in demanding conditions.</p></p>
