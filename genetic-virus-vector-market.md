<p><p>The genetic virus vector market comprises several key types. Retroviral vectors integrate their genetic material into the host genome, facilitating stable long-term expression. Adenoviral vectors, known for their efficiency, do not integrate but deliver genes effectively for transient expression. Adeno-associated viral vectors are popular for their safety and ability to infect non-dividing cells, providing long-lasting effects. Additionally, other market segments include lentiviral, vesicular stomatitis virus, and herpes simplex virus vectors, catering to diverse research and therapeutic applications.</p></p>
