<p><strong>The Carrier Screening Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Molecular Screening Test</li><li>Biochemical Screening Test</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Carrier screening is crucial for identifying individuals who may pass on genetic disorders to offspring. The market is primarily segmented into two types: molecular screening tests and biochemical screening tests. Molecular screening tests analyze DNA to detect specific mutations associated with genetic conditions, while biochemical screening tests evaluate metabolites or enzymes in bodily fluids to identify potential carrier status. Both testing types play a significant role in reproductive planning and risk assessment for hereditary diseases.</p></p>
