<p><p>Dynamic glass refers to advanced glazing technologies that change opacity or tint in response to environmental conditions or user input. The SPD (Suspended Particle Device) market utilizes particles suspended in a fluid that align to control light and heat. PDLC (Polymer Dispersed Liquid Crystal) technology enables switchable transparency by altering liquid crystal alignment. Electrochromic glass changes color or opacity through electrical signals, providing energy-efficient control over light and privacy. Each type offers unique benefits for commercial and residential applications.</p></p>
