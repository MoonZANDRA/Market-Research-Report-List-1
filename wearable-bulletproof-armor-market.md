<p><p>The wearable bulletproof armor market comprises two main types: soft bulletproof armor and hard bulletproof armor. Soft armor is typically made from flexible materials like Kevlar and is designed for comfort and mobility, offering protection against lower-caliber threats. In contrast, hard armor utilizes rigid materials such as ceramic or steel plates to provide enhanced protection against high-caliber firearms and armor-piercing rounds, although it is bulkier and less flexible. Both types cater to different needs in personal protection scenarios.</p></p>
