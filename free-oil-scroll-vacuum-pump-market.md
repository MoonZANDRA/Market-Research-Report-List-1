<p><p>The Free-oil Scroll Vacuum Pump market includes two primary types: single-sided and double-sided scroll pumps. Single-sided scroll pumps utilize a single spiraled element to compress gas, ideal for applications requiring lower vacuum levels and minimal maintenance. In contrast, double-sided scroll pumps feature two intertwined spirals, providing higher efficiency and deeper vacuum capabilities, making them suitable for more demanding industrial processes. Both types are valued for their oil-free operation, ensuring cleaner environments and lower operating costs, appealing to various sectors like pharmaceuticals and semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
