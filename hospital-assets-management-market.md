<p><p>Hospital asset management encompasses two main market types: cloud-based and on-premise systems. Cloud-based solutions offer flexibility, enabling real-time access to asset information from anywhere, reducing the need for extensive IT infrastructure. These systems often provide scalable features and automatic updates. In contrast, on-premise systems are installed locally within the hospital's infrastructure, offering greater control over data security and customization. However, they require higher upfront investment and ongoing maintenance. Both types aim to enhance efficiency, reduce costs, and improve asset utilization.</p></p>
