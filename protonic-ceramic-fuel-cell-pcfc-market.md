<p><p>Protonic Ceramic Fuel Cells (PCFC) operate based on proton conduction, offering high efficiency and fuel flexibility. Various types include Polymer Electrolyte Membrane Fuel Cells (PEMFC), which use a solid polymer for electrolyte; Direct Methanol Fuel Cells (DMFC), which directly utilize methanol; Phosphoric Acid Fuel Cells (PAFC), operating at higher temperatures; Solid Oxide Fuel Cells (SOFC), using a ceramic electrolyte for high efficiency; Molten Carbonate Fuel Cells (MCFC), utilizing molten carbonate salts; and Alkaline Fuel Cells (AFC), using an alkaline electrolyte, each serving diverse applications across the energy sector.</p></p>
