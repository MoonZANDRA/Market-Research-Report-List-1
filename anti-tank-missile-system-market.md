<p><p>The Anti-Tank Missile System market is categorized into two primary types: Man-Portable Anti-Tank Missiles (MPATMs) and Vehicle-Mounted Anti-Tank Missiles (VMATMs). MPATMs are lightweight, shoulder-fired weapons designed for infantry use, offering flexibility and mobility in combat. In contrast, VMATMs are integrated into armored vehicles and provide enhanced firepower and protection, allowing for greater range and accuracy against armored threats. Both systems are essential in modern warfare, catering to different operational needs on the battlefield.</p></p>
