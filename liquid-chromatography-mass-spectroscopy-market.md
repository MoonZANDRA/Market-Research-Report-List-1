<p><strong>The Liquid Chromatography-Mass Spectroscopy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>The Liquid Chromatography-Mass Spectrometry (LC-MS) market comprises several key types. Triple Quadrupole LC-MS is renowned for its sensitivity and quantitative capabilities, making it ideal for trace analysis. Time of Flight (TOF) LC-MS offers high resolution and rapid analysis, suitable for complex mixtures. Quadrupole-Time of Flight (Q-TOF) combines both techniques, providing accurate mass measurements and structural information. Other types may include Ion Trap and Orbitrap technologies, catering to specialized applications in pharmaceuticals, environmental testing, and proteomics.</p></p>
