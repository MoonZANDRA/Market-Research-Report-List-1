<p><p>The gas separation membrane filtration market is categorized into three main types: hollow fiber, spiral wound, and others. Hollow fiber membranes consist of thin fibers that maximize surface area for efficient gas separation, making them suitable for various applications. Spiral wound membranes feature a rolled design, enhancing compactness and facilitating high flow rates, ideal for large-scale separation processes. Other types include Flat sheet and tubular membranes, which cater to specialized applications based on specific separation requirements. Each type plays a crucial role in improving efficiency and performance in gas separation technologies.</p></p>
