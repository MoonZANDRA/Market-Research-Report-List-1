<p><p>The Aircraft On-Board Detectors Market is segmented into two main types: Fixed Type and Portable Type. Fixed Type detectors are permanently installed within the aircraft, offering continuous monitoring for specific threats like smoke or gas leaks, ensuring immediate response through integrated systems. In contrast, Portable Type detectors are handheld devices that can be used by crew members for on-the-spot inspections and emergencies, providing flexibility and ease of use during flight operations. Both types enhance safety and situational awareness in aviation.</p></p>
