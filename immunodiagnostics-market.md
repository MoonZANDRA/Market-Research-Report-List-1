<p><ul><li>Immunodiagnostic Instruments</li><li>Immunoassays</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The immunodiagnostics market comprises two main types: immunodiagnostic instruments and immunoassays. Immunodiagnostic instruments include devices such as analyzers and platforms that facilitate the detection and quantification of specific biomarkers in biological samples. Immunoassays refer to the tests themselves, employing antigen-antibody reactions to identify diseases or conditions by measuring concentrations of target analytes. Both segments are essential in clinical laboratories for disease diagnosis, monitoring, and therapeutic decision-making, enhancing patient care through accurate and timely results.</p></p>
