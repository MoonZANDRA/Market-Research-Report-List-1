<p><p>The hypervisor market is categorized into two primary types based on its virtualization focus: hardware and software. Hypervisors that virtualize computer hardware create multiple virtual machines on a single physical server, enabling efficient resource utilization and isolation among applications. Conversely, hypervisors that virtualize computer software facilitate the execution of applications in isolated environments, allowing for streamlined development, testing, and deployment. Together, these hypervisors enhance flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness in IT infrastructure management.</p></p>
