<p><p>The cable duct market is primarily categorized into flexible and rigid cable ducts. Flexible cable ducts are designed to accommodate bending and repositioning, making them ideal for places with complex layouts or frequent changes. They are typically made from materials like plastic or rubber. Rigid cable ducts, on the other hand, provide structural support and are utilized in environments requiring stable cable pathways, often featuring materials such as metal or hard plastic. Both types ensure organized cable management and protection.</p></p>
