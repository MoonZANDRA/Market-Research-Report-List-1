<p><p>Dental phosphor screen scanners are essential in dental imaging, categorized primarily into conventional and digital types. In contrast, digital scanners directly convert the captured images into digital format, offering faster processing and instant access to images. Both types cater to different practice needs, affecting workflow efficiency and diagnostic capabilities in dental settings.</p></p>
