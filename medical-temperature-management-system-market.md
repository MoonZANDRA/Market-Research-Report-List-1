<p><strong>The Medical Temperature Management System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Patient Warming Systems</li><li>Patient Cooling Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Medical Temperature Management System market is categorized into Patient Warming Systems and Patient Cooling Systems. Patient Warming Systems are designed to maintain normothermia in patients undergoing surgeries or suffering from trauma, utilizing technologies like forced-air warming or conductive warming blankets. Conversely, Patient Cooling Systems help reduce body temperature in conditions like hyperthermia, facilitating recovery and preventing complications; they include devices such as cooling blankets, cooling packs, and intravascular cooling systems. Both types aim to enhance patient outcomes in clinical settings.</p></p>
