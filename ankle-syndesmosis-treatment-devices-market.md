<p><p>The ankle syndesmosis treatment devices market primarily consists of two main types: suture button fixation and screw fixation. </p><p>Suture button fixation employs small buttons threaded with strong sutures to stabilize the fibula and tibia, allowing for dynamic stabilization and promoting natural healing. </p><p>In contrast, screw fixation involves the use of screws to directly stabilize the syndesmosis, providing rigid fixation. Both methods aim to restore the integrity and function of the ankle joint while minimizing complications and promoting recovery.</p></p>
