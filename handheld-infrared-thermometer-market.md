<p><strong>The Handheld Infrared Thermometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Forehead Type Thermometer</li><li>Ear Type Thermometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The handheld infrared thermometer market is primarily divided into two types: forehead and ear thermometers. Forehead type thermometers measure temperature from the temporal artery, providing a quick, non-invasive reading ideal for all age groups. They are often preferred in settings like schools and clinics. Ear type thermometers use infrared technology to gauge the temperature inside the ear canal, delivering accurate results, particularly for infants and children. Both types are widely used for fever detection and monitoring health.</p></p>
