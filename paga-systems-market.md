<p><p>PA/GA systems encompass various market types, notably Traditional Pressure Broadcasting and Network Broadcasting Systems. Traditional Pressure Broadcasting relies on centralized loudspeaker systems to deliver announcements in public spaces, ensuring wide coverage using acoustic principles. In contrast, Network Broadcasting Systems leverage digital technology and internet connectivity to distribute audio content over local or wide-area networks, allowing for more flexible, interactive, and scalable communication solutions. Both systems serve distinct needs in public address and general announcement applications.</p></p>
