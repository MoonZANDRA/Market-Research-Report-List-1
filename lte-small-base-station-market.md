<p><strong>The LTE Small Base Station Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Time Division Duplex (TDD)</li><li>Frequency Division Duplex (FDD)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LTE small base station market is primarily divided into two types: Time Division Duplex (TDD) and Frequency Division Duplex (FDD). TDD utilizes a single frequency channel for both uplink and downlink transmission, alternating between sending and receiving signals, making it efficient for dynamic data traffic. In contrast, FDD employs separate frequency bands for uplink and downlink, facilitating simultaneous transmission, which is beneficial for more consistent bandwidth demands. Both technologies cater to varying network requirements and user densities.</p></p>
