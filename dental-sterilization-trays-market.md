<p><strong>The Dental Sterilization Trays Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Normal Tray</li><li>Mesh Tray</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental sterilization trays market comprises two primary types: normal trays and mesh trays. Normal trays are solid, enclosed trays designed to hold dental instruments securely during sterilization processes, ensuring effective sterilant exposure. In contrast, mesh trays feature a perforated design that allows for improved airflow and drainage, enhancing the sterilization efficacy by minimizing moisture retention. Both types cater to the needs of dental professionals, promoting infection control and ensuring the availability of clean instruments for patient care.</p></p>
