Blood pressure cuffs are used to measure arterial pressure and monitor cardiovascular health. Cuffed endotracheal tubes secure the airway during ventilation, preventing aspiration and allowing controlled breathing in patients. Each type serves critical roles in patient care and respiratory management.</p></p>
