<p><p>The postpartum depression therapeutics market comprises various medication types including Selective Serotonin Reuptake Inhibitors (SSRIs) which enhance serotonin levels, providing relief from depression. Serotonin Norepinephrine Reuptake Inhibitors (SNRIs) target both serotonin and norepinephrine, offering a broader approach to treatment. Tricyclic Antidepressants (TCAs) are older medications that affect multiple neurotransmitters. Monoamine Oxidase Inhibitors (MAOIs) inhibit the enzyme that breaks down neurotransmitters, thus increasing their availability. Each class presents distinct mechanisms and side effects, catering to diverse patient needs.</p></p>
