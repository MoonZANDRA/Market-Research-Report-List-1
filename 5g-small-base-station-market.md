<p><ul><li>SA (Stand Alone)</li><li>NSA (Non-Stand Alone)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 5G Small Base Station market primarily features two types: Stand Alone (SA) and Non-Stand Alone (NSA). SA systems operate independently with a dedicated 5G core, enhancing network capabilities and low latency. In contrast, NSA relies on existing 4G infrastructure for control functions while utilizing 5G technology for data transmission, making it easier for operators to upgrade. The choice between SA and NSA impacts deployment speed, costs, and performance, catering to different market needs and technological readiness.</p></p>
